About the shop

A local workshop, not a middleman

Clearline Glass Works cuts, tempers, and fits every job ourselves — from a single balcony railing to a full rooftop glass structure. That means one point of contact, from measurement to final polish.

  • In-house tempering & cutting — no third-party job work, so timelines and quality stay in our control.
  • Site measurement before any quote — rates below are starting points; final pricing follows a free visit.
  • IS-certified toughened glass — every sheet tested for safety before it leaves the yard.
  • Warranty on fittings — SS & aluminium hardware covered for 2 years against defects.

01 — Balcony & staircase

Glass Railing

Frameless toughened glass railings fitted with stainless steel spider clamps or U-channel base fixing — for balconies, staircases, terraces & pool decks. Handrail finish in SS or wood-capped options available.

  • Glass12mm toughened, clear or frosted
  • Fitting hardwareSS 304 spider clamps / U-channel
  • HandrailSS round pipe or teak wood cap
  • Edge finishPencil polish, all exposed edges
Fitting typeGlass specRate (starting)
Spider-fit (frameless)12mm toughened, clear₹550/sqft*
U-channel base fixing10mm toughened, clear₹450/sqft*
Frosted / tinted band12mm toughened, frosted₹650/sqft*
*Rates are starting prices for standard clear toughened glass and do not include handrail cost, transport for far sites, or design cutouts. Confirmed after free measurement visit.
02 — Partitions & frontage

Tempered Glass Works

Toughened glass for shopfronts, office partitions, frameless doors & table tops. Includes cutouts for locks, patch fittings & floor springs for frameless doors.

  • Glass8mm–12mm toughened, clear/frosted/tinted
  • Door hardwareFloor spring, patch fittings, handles
  • Partition frameAluminium slim frame (optional, frameless also available)
  • Film finishFrosted vinyl film on request
ApplicationGlass specRate (starting)
Office partition (framed)8mm toughened, clear₹380/sqft*
Frameless door / shopfront12mm toughened, clear₹550/sqft*
Table top (polished edge)10mm toughened, clear₹420/sqft*
*Rates exclude aluminium framing (if chosen), floor springs for heavy doors, and frosted film application — quoted separately based on design.
03 — Skylights & pergola roofing

Rooftop Tempered Glass Fitting

Laminated toughened glass for rooftop skylights, pergola canopies & terrace roofing — built for weather exposure with UV-protective interlayer and a slope for water runoff.

  • Glass12mm toughened + PVB laminated (2-layer)
  • StructureAluminium/MS rafters, powder-coated
  • SealingStructural silicone, weatherproof gasket
  • UV protectionUV-blocking PVB interlayer
Structure typeGlass specRate (starting)
Flat skylight (fixed)12mm toughened + laminated₹700/sqft*
Sloped rooftop roofing12mm toughened + laminated + UV₹850/sqft*
Pergola canopy10mm toughened + laminated₹600/sqft*
*Rates depend heavily on height/access, structural framing material, and slope. Rooftop jobs always require a site visit before final quote — access and load-bearing checks matter here more than any other work type.
