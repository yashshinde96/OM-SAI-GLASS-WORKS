About the shop

A local workshop, not a middleman

Om Sai Glass cuts, tempers, and fits every job ourselves — from a single balcony railing to a full rooftop glass structure. That means one point of contact, from measurement to final polish.

  • In-house tempering & cutting — no third-party job work, so timelines and quality stay in our control.
  • Site measurement before any quote — every job is confirmed after a free visit.
  • IS-certified toughened glass — every sheet tested for safety before it leaves the yard.
  • Warranty on fittings — SS & aluminium hardware covered for 2 years against defects.

Our works

Every job, broken down honestly

Photos and materials for each type of work we do. Final specifications depend on site access, design complexity & glass thickness — confirmed after a free site visit.

01 — Balcony & staircase

Glass Railing

Frameless toughened glass railings fitted with stainless steel spigot or U-channel base fixing — for balconies, staircases, terraces & pool decks. Handrail finish in SS or wood-capped options available.

  • Glass12mm toughened, clear, laminated or frosted
  • Fitting hardwareSS 304 spigot, Aluminium spigot, Bracket and cover system, Bracket system and Continous profile
  • HandrailSS handle 40x40mm or 50x50mm and Aluminium handles
Fitting typeGlass spec
SS railing12mm toughened, clear, tinted, frosted, laminated
Continious profile fitting12mm toughened, clear, tinted, frosted, laminated
Aluminium spigot fitting12mm toughened, clear, tinted, frosted, laminated
Final handrail choice, hardware finish, and any design cutouts are confirmed after a free measurement visit.
02 — Partitions & frontage

Tempered Glass Works

Toughened glass for shopfronts, office partitions & frameless doors. Includes cutouts for locks, patch fittings & floor springs for frameless doors.

  • Glass8mm–12mm toughened, clear/frosted/tinted
  • Door hardwareFloor spring, patch fittings, handles
  • Partition frameAluminium slim frame (optional, frameless also available)
  • Film finishFrosted vinyl film on request
ApplicationGlass spec
Office partition (framed/frameless)8mm toughened, clear
Frameless door / shopfront12mm toughened, clear
Framing, floor springs for heavy doors, and film application are discussed and confirmed on-site.
03 — Skylights & pergola roofing

Rooftop Tempered Glass Fitting

Laminated toughened glass for rooftop skylights, pergola canopies & terrace roofing — built for weather exposure with UV-protective interlayer and a slope for water runoff.

  • Glass12mm toughened + PVB laminated (2-layer)
  • StructureAluminium/MS rafters, powder-coated
  • SealingStructural silicone, weatherproof gasket
  • UV protectionUV-blocking PVB interlayer
Structure typeGlass spec
Flat skylight (fixed)12mm toughened + laminated
Sloped rooftop roofing12mm toughened + laminated + UV
Pergola canopy10mm toughened + laminated
Height/access, framing material, and slope are confirmed during a mandatory site visit before work begins.
